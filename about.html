Our Story

Built on Reliability.
Grown on Results.

Since 1990, Direct Connection has served more than 500 clients across industries including higher education, government, non-profits, insurance, real estate, and financial services.

We provide full-service print and direct mail marketing, operating from our La Verne, California production facility. That means when you work with us, you get the speed, quality control, and accountability that comes from having every step of the process under one roof.

We're not a middleman. We're the production team that makes it happen.
